OCU MENTCARE

OCU MentCare is a mental health program at Oklahoma City University. It offers counseling, workshops, and support groups to help students with stress, anxiety, and other mental health issues. A 24/7 chatbot is available for quick mental health guidance and to schedule or manage appointments with counselors. The program also provides crisis intervention services for emergencies.

Jahnavi ms neurologist

A neurologist is a doctor who specializes in treating conditions related to the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. They help people with a variety of health issues. For example, they treat stroke, which happens when blood flow to the brain is blocked, and epilepsy, a condition that causes seizures. Neurologists also care for patients with multiple sclerosis (MS), a disease where the immune system attacks the nerves, and Parkinson's disease, which affects movement, causing tremors and stiffness

Geethika ms dermatologist

A dermatologist is a doctor who specializes in treating problems related to the skin, hair, and nails. They help people with a variety of issues. For example, they treat acne, which can affect teens and adults, and manage conditions like eczema and psoriasis, which cause red, itchy skin. Dermatologists also handle skin infections caused by bacteria, viruses, or fungi. They help those experiencing hair loss and can diagnose conditions like alopecia. A critical part of their work is checking for skin cancer; they examine moles and growths to ensure they are not harmful and can remove any suspicious ones. In addition to treating skin diseases, dermatologists offer solutions to reduce wrinkles and signs of aging through creams, lasers, or fillers.
